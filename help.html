How to help?

Dispose of Trash Correctly and Recycle Right: This seems simple, but a gust of wind can turn an overflowing bin into a source of pollution. Always secure your trash and recycling. Crucially, take a moment to learn your local recycling rules. "Wish-cycling" (tossing non-recyclable items in the bin) can contaminate an entire batch of recycling, sending it all to the landfill where it's more likely to leak into the environment.

Join a Cleanup: Participate in a local beach, river, or park cleanup. This is a direct, hands-on way to stop waste in its tracks. It's also a powerful reminder of what we're up against and can be a great community-building activity. If you can't find a local event, even spending 10 minutes picking up trash during your own walk can make a real difference.
